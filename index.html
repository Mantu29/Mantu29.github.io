Multivariate Extreme Value Theory Rare-Event Simulation ML for Finance Quantitative Risk Management Portfolio Optimization

I research at the intersection of financial mathematics, machine learning, and risk management — focusing on modeling multivariate extremes by integrating deep generative models with Extreme Value Theory. My work aims to build robust risk assessment methodologies for data-scarce scenarios such as financial crises and extreme natural events. Previously, I was a researcher at TCS Research, where I developed ML algorithms for image processing and filed multiple patents. I enjoy working on real-world problems and am always open to collaborations across academia and industry, particularly in areas involving risk modeling, quantitative finance, and applied machine learning.
